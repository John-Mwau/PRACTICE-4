J&M Farm limited

J&M Farm was established in 2020 with a purpose of contributing to food security across the Globe. The farm is engaged in commercial farming with a wide market reach both locally and internationally.At J&M we are transforming agriculture by adopting new technologies blended through research and innovation. We are geared towards transformative agriculture that cultivates quality health while empowering our communities.

Our Vision

Excellence in crop and livestock production enhanced through research and technology.

Our Mission

To conduct agricultural research through research, innovation, and technology for sustainable agricultural development while combating food insecurity.

Goal

To contribute to the growth of the agricultural sector through research coordination and regulation; technology and innovation development; and catalyzing transfer and utilization of agricultural research outputs.

Mandate

The objective of J&M Farm is to;

(a)To promote crop and animal production through research and innovation;

(b)To promote combat food insecurity;

(c) To empower small scale farmers access information, new technologies, quality breeds.
